About Me

I'm a high school student from Minnesota, passionate about learning and balancing academic pursuits with leisure activities. My academic interests are diverse, but I particularly excel in reading and mathematics. I enjoy getting lost in a good book or solving a challenging math problem. Im particularly interested in going into the medical field if I attend college. I find biology and science interesting, but I'm open to many of the majors in the STEM field. One of my ideal career choices would be Dentistry, but I also like the idea of becoming an Engineer.

When I'm not hitting the books, you'll often find me playing video games or sports. I love the strategic thinking, problem-solving, and creativity that many games require. During the summer months, I dedicate my time to volunteering at a local camp. This experience has been incredibly rewarding, allowing me to give back to my community while developing leadership skills and making lasting memories.

I've always been really interested in technology and the code behind it, so creating this page was a way for me to explore code and learn more about my interests.
